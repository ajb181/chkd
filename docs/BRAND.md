## The Contract Philosophy

**chkd is NOT an automation tool.**

Expect to interact. Expect to guide. Expect to say "don't do that, do it this way."

### What this means:

1. **It's a contract, not a cage** — Both parties (you and AI) agree to work a certain way. Neither is forced. Both benefit.

2. **You hold up your end** — The AI follows the workflow, but YOU need to:
   - Stay engaged, not walk away
   - Guide AI when it drifts ("use this tool", "focus on that")
   - Verify before ticking off
   - Log bugs when you see them

3. **Imperfect by design** — We could make it rigid and foolproof. But rigid = unenjoyable. The flexibility is the feature. The trade-off is you stay involved.

4. **Your intellect matters** — chkd doesn't replace your judgment. It creates space for it. You're still the one deciding if something is right, if the approach makes sense, if AI should try differently.

5. **It's collaborative, not hands-off** — If you want "set it and forget it" automation, chkd isn't for you. If you want AI as a capable partner you work WITH, this is it.

### The honest truth:

AI drifts. **You drift too.** That's human nature — we all want to skip steps, batch work, "just get it done."

chkd keeps BOTH of you structured:
- Forced checkpoints where you realign
- Neither party can skip the verification
- Small course corrections, not big rewrites

**Your job:** Stay engaged, catch drift early, guide when needed.
**AI's job:** Follow the workflow, stay focused, be transparent.

**Together:** You ship good software, one verified tick at a time.

## The Real Value Proposition

### What chkd actually does:

1. **Brings the human IN** — You're not watching from the sidelines. You're engaged, guiding, deciding at every step.

2. **Framework for YOUR thinking** — The structure helps you clarify what you actually want before and during building. Forces good questions.

3. **Verification is empowerment** — Each checkpoint is YOU confirming "yes, this is right" or "no, let's adjust." You're in the driver's seat.

4. **AI supports YOUR thinking** — AI isn't replacing your judgment. It's amplifying your vision. You think, it builds. You verify, it refines.

5. **Better thinking → Better output** — When you're clear about what you want, AI can deliver exactly that. Garbage in, garbage out. Clarity in, quality out.

6. **Multi-agent coordination** — Real work often needs multiple agents: one researching, one building, one reviewing. Without structure, they duplicate work, contradict each other, and create chaos. chkd gives you the coordination layer to manage multiple agents in one workflow.

### What we're NOT saying:
- ❌ "Slow down AI" — We're not anti-speed
- ❌ "Control AI" — Sounds restrictive, bureaucratic
- ❌ "Discipline AI" — Makes AI sound like a problem child
- ❌ "AI makes mistakes" — Negative framing

### What we ARE saying:
- ✓ "Think clearly, build confidently"
- ✓ "Your vision, AI's speed"
- ✓ "Stay engaged, ship faster"
- ✓ "A framework that makes both of you better"
